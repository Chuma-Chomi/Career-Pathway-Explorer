What is Staff/Teacher Mode?
This mode is for school staff and teachers. It allows you to:
  • Upload or enter ECZ Grade 7 results for multiple learners
  • Analyze and place learners into suitable career pathways automatically
  • Download or print placement reports for your records
  • Access a summary of overall school placements and trends
Note: Only authorized staff should use this mode.
What is Learner/Guardian Mode?
This mode is for learners and guardians. It allows you to:
  • Enter your ECZ Grade 7 results to see career pathway recommendations
  • Take a career interest quiz to discover suitable fields
  • Explore information about various Zambian career paths
  • View and save your career pathway report
No login is required for learners or guardians.
What does Accessibility Options do?
This feature helps users who may have difficulty seeing small text or certain colors. You can:
  • Increase the text size for easier reading
  • Switch the portal to a high-contrast mode for better visibility
Click the button multiple times to cycle through these options.
